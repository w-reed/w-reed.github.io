Echinacea Project

From 2014 to 2018 I worked on the Echinacea Project investigating habitat fragmentation in western Minnesota.

We studied a broad range of projects from plant pollinator interactions to population demographics in a highly fragmented landscape.

Healthy Prairies Project

At the University of Minnesota I worked as a research assistant on the Healthy Prairies Project. Which aims to quantify the scale of local adaptation in native prairie species as well as to understand the influence of microbes on adaptation.
